Company Bio

Founded in 1754 by Jamo Pompelo , Pens & Pencils Co. Ltd. was birthed from a young entrepeneur's dream to see others enjoy the process of writing as much as he did. "It's all magic, i think" was one of Pompelo's favourite lines as he spent most of his spare time writing poems, short essays and newspaper articles. And now almost two centuries later, he is still alive today making these fine hand-crafted writing instruments.
